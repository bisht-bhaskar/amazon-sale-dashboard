# amazon-sale-dashboard
Amazon Product Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing $2.18M YTD sales and 27.75K products sold to track business performance.

Designed KPI cards to monitor YTD Sales, QTD Sales ($811K), Products Sold, and 19.42M customer reviews for executive-level reporting.

Performed sales trend analysis (monthly & weekly) to identify Q4 peak season growth and demand spikes (Week 40–50).

Analyzed product category performance, identifying Men Shoes (43% revenue share) as the top-performing category.

Built dynamic filters (Product Category, Quarter) enabling drill-down analysis and improved decision-making.

Identified top-selling and most-reviewed products to support inventory planning, marketing strategy, and cross-selling opportunities.

Applied data modeling, DAX measures, and data visualization best practices to create a business-ready analytical solution
